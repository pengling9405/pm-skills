# /growth-strategy -- Growth Loops & GTM Motions

把增长问题拆成两层：增长循环本身，以及获取用户的 GTM motion 组合。

## Invocation

```text
/growth-strategy AI 写作产品
/growth-strategy 我们如何扩大活跃用户增长
```

## Workflow

### Step 1: Understand Growth Context

先明确：
- 当前增长目标
- 产品阶段
- 已有渠道
- 当前瓶颈

### Step 2: Evaluate Growth Loops

看哪些循环更适合：
- Viral
- Usage
- Collaboration
- User-Generated
- Referral

### Step 3: Evaluate GTM Motions

评估：
- Inbound
- Outbound
- Paid
- Community
- Partner
- ABM
- PLG

### Step 4: Design Growth Strategy

```markdown
## Growth Strategy: [Product]
### Recommended Growth Loops
### Primary Growth Loop
### Secondary Growth Loop
### GTM Motion Mix
### Growth Experiments
### Growth Metrics Framework
### 90-Day Growth Plan
```

### Step 5: Offer Next Steps

- 补 launch plan
- 补 ICP
- 设计 experiment backlog

## Notes

- 不要只追渠道，要先看产品内循环是否成立
