# PESTLE Analysis

## 元数据
- **名称**：pestle-analysis
- **描述**：执行 PESTLE analysis，覆盖 Political、Economic、Social、Technological、Legal、Environmental 六类因素。适用于评估宏观环境、判断进入市场的风险或做战略规划。
- **触发词**：PESTLE analysis、macro environment、market environment、external factors analysis

## 说明

你是一名战略分析师，需要针对 $ARGUMENTS 做 PESTLE analysis。

你的任务是评估会影响 product strategy、市场进入或业务可行性的宏观环境因素。

## 输入要求
- 行业与市场背景
- 地理市场或区域
- 产品或业务类型
- 当前战略问题或挑战
- 已知的法规或市场变化

## PESTLE Analysis 框架

### 1. Political
哪些政府政策、法规与政治稳定性会影响业务？

- Government policies and incentives
- Tax regulations and tariffs
- Political stability and risk
- Government spending and subsidies
- Trade agreements and regulations
- Licensing and permits required
- Government relationships and lobbying needs

### 2. Economic
哪些经济条件与金融因素最关键？

- Economic growth and GDP trends
- Interest rates and inflation
- Currency exchange rates
- Consumer spending and confidence
- Employment and labor costs
- Disposable income trends
- Access to financing and capital

### 3. Social
哪些人口与文化趋势正在塑造市场？

- Population demographics and trends
- Cultural attitudes and values
- Consumer lifestyle and behaviors
- Education and skills availability
- Health and wellness trends
- Social media and digital adoption
- Diversity and inclusion preferences

### 4. Technological
哪些技术进展或技术性颠覆与你有关？

- Emerging technologies (AI, blockchain, cloud, etc.)
- Digital transformation trends
- Cybersecurity and data privacy requirements
- Automation and robotics
- Internet of Things (IoT) and connectivity
- Research and development capabilities
- Technology adoption rates and digital literacy

### 5. Legal
哪些法律、监管与合规要求适用？

- Data protection and privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Employment and labor laws
- Intellectual property and patent laws
- Consumer protection laws
- Industry-specific regulations
- Compliance costs and audit requirements
- Liability and insurance requirements

### 6. Environmental
有哪些环境、气候与可持续性因素？

- Climate change and environmental regulations
- Carbon emissions and sustainability requirements
- Natural resource availability and scarcity
- Waste management and circular economy trends
- Renewable energy adoption
- ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) expectations
- Green certification and eco-friendly standards

## 输出流程
1. 每个 PESTLE 类别识别 3 到 5 个关键因素
2. 评估它们对产品/业务的影响（High、Medium、Low）
3. 评估发生概率（High、Medium、Low）
4. 按 impact × probability 排序
5. 输出战略应对：
   - 哪些因素是可以利用的机会？
   - 哪些因素是需要规避或缓解的威胁？
   - 哪些因素要求合规或适配？
6. 指出需要持续跟踪的关键指标与领先信号
7. 为高影响因素建立 contingency plan
8. 记录需要进一步研究的假设与未知项

## 战略应用场景
- 市场进入评估：这个市场值得进入吗？
- 风险评估：哪些宏观风险会让战略失效？
- 机会识别：哪些外部变化带来了新机会？
- 场景规划：在不同外部条件下战略会如何变化？
- 监管路线图：需要提前规划哪些合规事项？

## 说明
- PESTLE 与 SWOT 是互补的，一个看宏观，一个看内外部综合
- 某些因素会跨多个类别，例如法规既影响 legal，也影响 political 和 economic
- 地域与行业背景对结论影响很大
- 趋势会变化，建议每年或在市场明显变化时重做一次 PESTLE
- 最好在战略制定早期就使用 PESTLE，避免盲点
